Lagoona Villa is a vacation rental property in Trinidad. The site needed to show the property at its best and give prospective guests a clear way to inquire about bookings.

## What was built

A WordPress site with a modified theme, focused on strong photography presentation and a simple booking inquiry flow. The layout was designed to give the property room to breathe — large images, clear availability information, and contact details that were easy to find.

## Features

- Property overview and room details
- Photo gallery
- Booking inquiry form
- Mobile-responsive layout

The site is still live at [lagoonavilla.com](https://www.lagoonavilla.com/).
