Night Owlz TT is a Trinidad-based nightlife and events brand. The site needed to serve a dual purpose: promote the brand and sell tickets or merchandise online.

## What was built

A WordPress e-commerce site with a custom theme built to reflect the brand's late-night energy. WooCommerce handled the store side, giving the client a manageable way to list products and process orders without needing a developer for every update.

## Key features

- Custom WordPress theme
- WooCommerce e-commerce integration
- Events and brand content sections
- Mobile-responsive layout
