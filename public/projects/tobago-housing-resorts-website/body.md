Tobago Housing Resorts is a hospitality property in Tobago. The site was built to give prospective guests a clear picture of the accommodation and facilities, and to drive direct booking inquiries.

## What was built

A WordPress site with a modified theme, built around strong photography and clear information architecture. Guests can browse accommodation options, view facilities, and reach out directly through a booking inquiry form.

## Key features

- Accommodation and facilities overview
- Photo galleries per property section
- Booking inquiry form
- Mobile-responsive layout
