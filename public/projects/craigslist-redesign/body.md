## Overview

This redesign explored what Craigslist could feel like with a clearer hierarchy, calmer spacing, and a more approachable browsing experience while preserving the fast, utilitarian nature of the original product.

## Highlights

- Reframed a dense legacy interface into a more readable layout
- Focused on search, category browsing, and listing clarity
- Used as a design exercise in improving usability without over-designing the product
