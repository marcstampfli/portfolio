Gypsea Soul Jewelry is a Trinidad-based jewelry brand. They needed a site that could sell online — not just a brochure, but a working store with checkout built in.

## What was built

The site runs on WordPress with WooCommerce for the shop. PayPal was integrated as the payment gateway, which made sense for a local T&T business selling to both local and international customers. The theme was modified to fit the brand's bohemian aesthetic — warm tones, clean product photography presentation, and a shopping experience that doesn't get in the way.

## Key features

- WooCommerce product catalog and cart
- PayPal checkout integration
- Mobile-responsive storefront
- Modified WordPress theme to match brand identity
