## Overview

This project served as a personal brand platform and portfolio hub. The work covered both the interface direction and the final WordPress implementation, with the goal of presenting selected projects through a stronger, more deliberate visual identity.

## Highlights

- Design direction explored before development and folded into the final site
- Custom WordPress theme tailored to a personal design language
- Combined portfolio content, brand positioning, and contact flow
- Designed to feel more editorial and intentional than a typical template site
