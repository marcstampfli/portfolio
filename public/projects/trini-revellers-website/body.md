Trini Revellers is a Trinidad Carnival band. They needed a site to promote the band during the lead-up to Carnival season — showcasing their costumes, mas camp details, and registration information.

## What was built

An early WordPress site designed around the Carnival calendar. The layout was built to handle seasonal content updates — new costume sections, announcements, and event details — without requiring a developer each time.

## Key features

- Costume section and band information
- Mas camp details and registration info
- WordPress CMS for seasonal updates
- Web design aligned with Carnival branding
